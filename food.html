Best SD Food

Prince St. Pizza

Address: 415 Market St, San Diego, CA 92101

Their signature pies, particularly the "Spicy Spring" with its distinctive cup-and-char pepperoni and spicy marinara, have garnered a cult following for their rich flavor profile, fresh ingredients, and perfectly crisp yet fluffy dough.

Board & Brew

Address: 4516 Mission Blvd, San Diego, CA 92109

Board & Brew is a popular sandwich shop known for its commitment to fresh, high-quality ingredients and a simple, straightforward menu. They specialize in generously portioned, made-from-scratch sandwiches, with a particular highlight being their beloved "Secret Sauce," a classified, scratch-made condiment that customers often get on the side for dipping

Tacos El Gordo

Address: 3265 Palm Ave, San Diego, CA 92154

Tacos El Gordo is a renowned purveyor of authentic Tijuana-style tacos, celebrated for its commitment to traditional flavors and fresh, high-quality ingredients. They are particularly famous for their handmade corn tortillas and thinly sliced, marinated "Adobada" (spiced pork) cooked on a vertical spit (al pastor), as well as their flavorful carne asada.
